## Organization Overview

**Guitars Over Guns** is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded in 2008 that empowers youth through music, mentorship, and the arts. They operate in Miami, Chicago, Los Angeles, NYC, and Las Vegas, serving 1,100+ students annually at 24 schools and 5 community studios with 60+ professional teaching artists.

**Key Programs:**
- School-based after-school programs (instrumental music, vocals, production, visual arts)
- Community-based programs (summer camps, alumni programs, recording studios)
- M-Power Mental Health Program
- Haven Studios (Chicago - recording studio)
- Haven Cinema (Chicago - videography/multimedia arts)

**Website Structure:** Extensive navigation with multiple regions, programs, events, impact reporting, giving options, team pages, and media coverage.

## 🚨 CRITICAL CONSTRAINTS

### Content Management & Updates

**This is the most important question of the entire call:**

**Q: How often do you need to update the website content, and what types of updates do you typically make?**

Follow-up clarifications:
- Do you add new events monthly/weekly?
- Do you update staff/team bios regularly?
- Do you add blog posts or news articles?
- Do you need to update program information seasonally?
- Who on your team will be making these updates?

**If frequent updates are needed (weekly/monthly):** 
"I want to be transparent upfront - we're building this as a custom coded website with micro-interactions and sophisticated design. This means content updates require developer knowledge. If you need to frequently update content yourself, this approach may not be the best fit. We'd need to either:
1. Build this in a CMS platform (WordPress, Webflow) that allows non-technical updates, OR
2. Establish a retainer relationship where we handle content updates for you"

**If infrequent updates (quarterly/annually):**
"Perfect - this approach will work well. We can handle periodic updates as separate projects or through a minimal retainer."

## Key Message to Communicate

"We're excited about the potential to build something truly unique for Guitars Over Guns. Our approach creates highly custom, visually stunning websites with sophisticated micro-interactions - essentially treating the website as a piece of digital art that reflects the creative, transformative work you do.

The tradeoff is that this approach is best suited for organizations that need periodic updates (quarterly or annually) rather than daily/weekly content changes. If you need frequent self-service updates, we'd recommend a different approach using a platform like Webflow.

But if you're looking for something that stands out, tells your story in a compelling way, and serves as a powerful tool for fundraising and recruitment - without the need for constant content updates - this is where we excel."
